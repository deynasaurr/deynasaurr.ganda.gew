BLOODY MARY

A Bloody Mary is a cocktail containing vodka, tomato juice, and other spices and flavorings.

COSMO

A cosmopolitan, or, informally, a cosmo, is a cocktail made with vodka, Cointreau, cranberry juice, and freshly squeezed or sweetened lime juice.

BLACK RUSSIAN

The Black Russian is made of two-ingredient cocktail that consists of vodka and coffee liqueur.

MOSCOW MULE

A Moscow mule is a cocktail made with vodka, ginger beer and lime juice, garnished with a slice or wedge of lime, and a sprig of mint.

VODKA TONIC

A vodka tonic is a long drink made with varying proportions of vodka and tonic water.
